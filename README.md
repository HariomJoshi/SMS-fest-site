# Culrav - Avishkar Festival Website

Welcome to the official website for the **Culrav - Avishkar Festival** hosted by the **School of Management Studies (SMS)** at **MNNIT Allahabad**. This website serves as a comprehensive platform for participants, organizers, and attendees to engage with all aspects of the festival, highlighting its events, themes, and activities.

## Table of Contents

- [Overview](#overview)
- [Festival Highlights](#festival-highlights)
- [Technologies Used](#technologies-used)

## Overview

The Culrav - Avishkar Festival is an annual event organized by SMS, MNNIT Allahabad, bringing together students and professionals to participate in a variety of competitive and educational activities. The festival is a platform for showcasing talent, sharing knowledge, and promoting creativity. 

### Fest Themes

- **Oligopoly**: A competition that focuses on strategic interactions among firms. Participants will engage in activities that test their business acumen and market strategy skills.
  
- **Monopoly**: This segment emphasizes individual prowess in managing a business, encouraging participants to think creatively and strategically.

## Festival Highlights

- **Engaging Workshops**: Attend workshops led by industry experts, providing valuable insights and skills relevant to the business landscape.
  
- **Competitions**: Participate in exciting competitions designed to challenge your intellect and creativity.
  
- **Networking Opportunities**: Meet and connect with like-minded individuals, industry professionals, and alumni.

- **Cultural Events**: Experience the vibrant culture of MNNIT through performances, exhibitions, and other artistic displays.

## Technologies Used

This website is built using the following technologies:

- **React**: A JavaScript library for building user interfaces.
- **Tailwind CSS**: A utility-first CSS framework for designing responsive and modern interfaces.
- **Framer Motion**: A library for creating animations in React applications.
- **Locomotive Scroll**: A smooth scrolling library for enhancing the user experience.
